Appendix B — Useful Mathematical Definitions and Tables

B.1 Definition of modulus for integers

Let \(n\in\mathbb{Z}\) and \(N \in \mathbb{N}\). The mod operator \[n \% N = \left\{ \begin{array}{cc} \text{remainder}\left(\frac{n}{N}\right) & n \geq 0\\ N - \text{remainder}\left(\frac{|n|}{N}\right) & n < 0 \end{array} \right.\] where \(\text{remainder}\) is the remainder after dividing \(n\) by \(N\).
